Interactive Job Application Checklist

  • Choose the appropriate resume
  • Edit the resume summary to fit the job app
  • Choose the correct cover letter template
  • Edit the cover letter to fit the job app
  • Read through for errors and verify info sounds good
  • Save the resume and cover letter as PDFs
  • Submit and choose the next job app to apply to
  • Do a stretch, pet Duke, or look at your phone before starting the next app
